# 🏙️ Urban Council Management System

## 📌 Project Overview
The Urban Council Management System is a web-based application developed to modernize and streamline the daily operations of an urban council. The system improves communication between citizens, private companies, and council staff while ensuring efficient service delivery and transparent management of municipal services.

This platform digitizes key council services such as public area booking, complaint handling, feedback management, and payment processing, allowing users to access services quickly and conveniently through a centralized digital system.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Digitize urban council services and reduce manual paperwork  
- Improve communication between citizens and council authorities  
- Provide efficient complaint and feedback handling  
- Enable secure online booking and payment services  
- Enhance transparency and accountability in council operations  

---

## 🧩 System Modules

## 👤 Citizen Module
- Citizen registration and login  
- Submit complaints to the council  
- Provide feedback on services  
- Book public areas (e.g., halls, community centers, grounds)  
- Make online payments for services  
- View payment and invoice history  

## 🏢 Private Company Module
- Company registration and profile management  
- Manage service contracts  
- Submit work progress reports  
- View assigned tasks from the council  

## 🧑‍💼 Admin / Staff Module
- Manage citizen and company records  
- Approve service requests and bookings  
- Schedule special waste collection services  
- Generate system reports and statistics  
- Monitor complaints and feedback
